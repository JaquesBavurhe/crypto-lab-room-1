Part 4: RSA Asymmetric Encryption
Exercise 4.1: RSA with Small Numbers

Given:
Public Key: (n = 33, e = 7)
Private Key: (n = 33, d = 3)
Message M = 2

RSA Encryption Formula:
C = M^e mod n

Step 1: Calculate M^e
2^7 = 128

Step 2: Apply modulo n
128 mod 33 = 29

Step 3: Ciphertext
C = 29

Final Answer:
The encrypted ciphertext is 29.
